    • Fire That Thing!

    Maya 2025

    Audio from Rambo 1 - First Blood (1982)

    "Ninja" Rig from BuiltByColossus. Backdrop from Sketchfab.

    This was a short character animation project to work on some body mechanics and dynamic expressions. After working through a first iteration, I ran the following version by my animation professor, who encouraged me to take a step back and really critique myself on my work.

    Taking time to do so, I came up with the following list of changes that needed to be made: Revisions Document

    Having the chance to review my own work and observe professional animation, I realized I was struggling with pushing my poses and I also needed to de-sync the character's movements throughout. Spending a month intensely critiquing my own work was invaluable for the way I think about animation. I'm really proud of the final result, and I hope to carry these lessons into future projects!
